# Job Tracker — Features & Benefits Reference

**What this document is:** A single, complete list of every feature currently built into Job Tracker, each paired with its primary benefit. It is written to be fed into other AI tools as source material for marketing copy, sales scripts, landing pages, ad copy, comparison charts, and product messaging.

**Product summary:** Job Tracker is an all-in-one field-service operations platform for home-services and trades companies. It runs the entire job lifecycle in one place — lead → schedule → job costing → production → permitting → time → payroll → commissions — with GPS asset tracking, vendor management, analytics, and deep CRM/accounting integrations. It is an installable web app (works on phone, tablet, and desktop), with real cloud login, company-level data isolation, and an agency mode for managing many companies from one account.

**How to read it:** Each line is `Feature — Benefit`. Features are grouped by product area.

---

## 1. Dashboard & Daily Operations

- **Executive dashboard with live KPI tiles (open pipeline, open job value, YTD revenue, YTD gross profit)** — Gives owners the state of the business at a glance the moment they log in, no report-building required.
- **Active jobs and hot leads shortlists with margin and value** — Surfaces what matters most today without digging through lists.
- **Tasks-needing-attention panel (overdue / due today)** — Stops work from slipping through the cracks between office and field.
- **Global search across jobs, leads, tasks, and notes (Ctrl/Cmd+K)** — Finds any record in seconds by name, address, phone, customer, or job number.
- **In-app notifications with unread badges and an inbox** — Keeps the team aligned on assignments and due dates without relying on email.
- **My Day mobile screen (today's appointments, site access, checklists, clock-in, tasks)** — Gives field crews everything for the day on one phone screen, cutting confused calls back to the office.
- **One-tap map directions and click-to-call from appointments** — Saves techs time and reduces late or missed arrivals.
- **Mobile photo capture and daily logs from the job site** — Documents work as it happens, creating a defensible record for disputes and QC.

## 2. Pipeline & Lead Management

- **Full lead tracking (contact, estimate, job type, PMs, source, proposal/invoice links, signed date)** — Keeps every opportunity organized so nothing is forgotten and follow-up is consistent.
- **Milestone checkboxes (call made, site visit scheduled, estimate offered)** — Makes the sales process visible and coachable at a glance.
- **Lead statuses (Active, Follow Up, Closed-Won, Not Interested)** — Shows exactly where every deal stands and what needs attention.
- **One-click convert lead → job** — Eliminates double data entry when a deal closes; everything carries forward.
- **Lost-job capture with reasons** — Turns lost deals into data that reveals why the company loses work.
- **Bulk CSV lead import with smart column mapping** — Gets an existing lead list into the system in minutes.
- **Commission tier set automatically at close by lead source** — Ensures reps are paid correctly with zero manual setup per deal.

## 3. Job Tracking & Job Costing

- **Job list and drag-and-drop Kanban board by stage** — Lets the whole team see and move work through the pipeline visually.
- **Job health badges (On track / Watch / At risk / Needs attention)** — Flags troubled jobs early so managers can intervene before they lose money.
- **Real-time profitability per job (value, cost, actual GP, live margin %)** — Shows the true margin on every job as work happens, not weeks later.
- **Automatic cost roll-up (labor at blended rate + subs + materials + consumables + equipment)** — Removes guesswork and manual spreadsheets from job costing.
- **Expected vs. actual gross profit with color-coded margins** — Instantly shows which jobs are beating or missing target.
- **Budget vs. actual tracking (labor $, labor hours, materials, overall) with progress bars** — Catches cost overruns while there's still time to act.
- **Customer payment schedule / draws (% or fixed, tied to stage or phase)** — Makes sure the company invoices and collects on time, protecting cash flow.
- **Production-readiness checklist that gates scheduling** — Prevents jobs from being scheduled before they're actually ready to start.
- **Delay tracking (reason, note, expected resolution)** — Documents why jobs stall and quantifies the impact.
- **"On My Way" customer text from the job** — Improves customer experience and cuts no-access trips.
- **Estimated vs. used production days** — Keeps multi-day jobs on schedule and exposes overruns.
- **Labor, subcontractor, material, and consumable cost entry with receipt/document upload** — Captures every dollar of cost against the right job, with proof attached.
- **Consumables inventory that auto-costs and decrements stock** — Accounts for small recurring materials that usually go untracked, and warns before running out.

## 4. Production, Quality & Documents

- **Production log (daily logs, weather, crew, work done, problems)** — Builds a complete job history for accountability and dispute protection.
- **QC checkpoints (pass/fail gates, photo-required, auto fix-it tasks on fail)** — Enforces quality standards and creates a paper trail of inspections.
- **Milestones with target dates** — Keeps long jobs on track against a plan.
- **Unified job timeline (stages, visits, delays, logs, milestones, notes, files)** — Tells the full story of a job in one scrollable feed.
- **Central document repository with key-document slots (site plan, permit, engineering, proposal, invoice)** — Puts the right paperwork one click away for the whole team.
- **Required-document workflow (Needed → Requested → Received → Approved)** — Ensures compliance paperwork is actually collected and signed off.
- **Photo gallery with categories, auto-synced from CRM and CompanyCam** — Keeps job-site photos organized and effortless to gather.
- **Two-way SMS and notes synced with the CRM contact** — Keeps all customer communication attached to the job, visible to the team.

## 5. Scheduling & Dispatch

- **Native appointment booking (types, colors, durations, statuses)** — Replaces separate scheduling tools; Job Tracker is the single source of truth.
- **Multi-day and recurring bookings** — Lays out long and repeating jobs in one action instead of booking day by day.
- **Crew, team, asset, and vendor assignment per visit with a designated foreman** — Makes sure every job has the right people and equipment, with clear ownership.
- **Live free/busy availability from connected calendars** — Prevents double-booking people when scheduling.
- **Conflict detection (hard block on asset double-booking, warnings on people/vendors)** — Stops physically impossible schedules before they're saved.
- **Job phases (e.g. Demo → Install → Inspection) with status tracking** — Organizes complex jobs into manageable, trackable stages.
- **Visit and foreman checklists** — Ensures nothing is missed on site and the crew lead is prepared.
- **Business scheduling board with "clocked-in-now" cost and at-risk-job panels** — Gives owners a live command view of the day's labor cost and problem jobs.
- **Crew teams (members, capacity, working days, skills)** — Enables smart crew-based dispatching and utilization planning.
- **Per-user Google Calendar and Outlook sync** — Lets each team member see work on the calendar they already use.
- **Calendar view (Day/Week/Month) with color-by-job and multi-day project bars** — Makes the whole schedule readable at a glance.
- **Customer appointment reminders (SMS via CRM) with a reminder schedule** — Reduces no-shows and last-minute cancellations.

## 6. Time Tracking

- **Project-based clock in/out with labor categories** — Ties every labor hour directly to a job for accurate costing.
- **Switch project or category without clocking out** — Matches how field crews actually work across multiple jobs in a day.
- **Auto clock-in when a tracked employee arrives on a geofenced job site** — Removes the friction (and forgotten punches) of manual clock-ins.
- **Live "clocked-in-now" view with per-person hours and projected payroll cost** — Shows labor spend in real time and who's working where.
- **Editable timesheets with configurable rounding and overnight-shift handling** — Keeps payroll accurate and correctable without breaking the record.
- **Role-gated time-clock editing** — Lets payroll/admins manage hours while techs only manage their own.

## 7. Spending & Field Cost Capture

- **One-tap mobile spending capture (materials or vendor payment) with receipt photo** — Lets field staff log costs the moment they happen, so nothing is lost.
- **Auto-post to job costing and auto-email receipts to payables** — Routes every expense to the right job and to bookkeeping automatically.
- **Recent spending feed and admin CSV export with receipt links** — Gives the office full visibility and easy reconciliation.

## 8. Commissions

- **Automatic commission engine (sales % on revenue, GP % on gross profit after sales)** — Calculates pay correctly every time, ending spreadsheet errors and disputes.
- **Rate tiers by lead source (company-generated vs. self-generated)** — Rewards reps appropriately for the leads they bring themselves.
- **Independent sales and GP splits across multiple PMs (even or custom, validated to 100%)** — Handles team-sold and team-run jobs fairly and transparently.
- **Payout schedules (at install, at sale, or 50/50 split)** — Aligns commission timing with the company's cash flow and policy.
- **Per-job bonuses for the running PM and every tech who logged labor** — Extends incentives to the field crew, not just salespeople.
- **Referral commissions to a named source** — Automates paying partners and customers who send work.
- **Approval workflow (recipients approve or request adjustments; email notifications)** — Creates a transparent, auditable sign-off before anyone is paid.
- **Draw tracker (earned vs. draws paid, net ahead/behind)** — Manages advances against commission cleanly, with no manual math.
- **Commission clawbacks on canceled/deleted paid jobs** — Recovers overpaid commissions automatically and correctly.
- **Commission forecast by stage** — Shows reps and owners expected payouts on open work.
- **Payroll-ready CSV export and one-click push to Gusto** — Turns commissions into a payroll run in seconds.

## 9. Payroll

- **Personal "My Pay" view for every team member** — Gives everyone transparency into their hours, commissions, bonuses, and draws.
- **Pay-period cadence (weekly / biweekly / monthly) with navigation** — Matches the company's real pay schedule.
- **Pace projection for the current period** — Motivates reps by showing what they're on track to earn.
- **Company payroll view with per-member totals** — Gives payroll staff one screen to prepare a run.
- **Gusto submit surface (preview and stage approved commissions)** — Loads payroll for the company to review and run — accurate, but never fired automatically.

## 10. Asset & GPS Tracking

- **Live map tracking of equipment, vehicles, and people** — Shows where everything and everyone is, in real time, on one map.
- **Three tracking methods (phone app, hardware GPS tracker, manual pin)** — Fits any budget and any asset, from a $0 phone app to dedicated hardware.
- **Per-asset data (location, battery, speed, odometer, service-due)** — Keeps tabs on the health and whereabouts of every asset.
- **Team location tracking (only while clocked in and opted in)** — Improves dispatch and accountability while respecting privacy.
- **Job-site geofences with auto clock-in** — Automates time tracking and confirms crews are actually on site.
- **Maintenance schedules by miles or days with due alerts** — Prevents costly breakdowns by keeping equipment serviced on time.
- **Job-site equipment billing (per visit or per hour on site)** — Captures equipment cost as job cost automatically, protecting margin.

## 11. Vendors & Subcontractors

- **Vendor micro-CRM (profiles, multiple contacts, categories)** — Keeps the whole subcontractor network organized and searchable.
- **Approval workflow and compliance document tracking (license, insurance, W-9, custom) with expiration** — Ensures you only dispatch vendors who are vetted and insured, reducing liability.
- **"X/Y required documents on file" approval gate** — Makes compliance status obvious and enforceable.
- **Two-way vendor messaging** — Keeps subcontractor communication in one place, tied to the record.
- **External vendor portal (self-service login across all client companies)** — Lets vendors upload their own docs, see their schedule, and submit photos — offloading admin work.
- **Vendor booking availability (weekdays/hours, warn or block)** — Prevents scheduling vendors when they can't work.
- **Per-booking job-site photo upload by the vendor** — Gets field documentation straight from subs into the job.

## 12. Permitting & Engineering

- **Staged permit board with aging alerts** — Keeps permits moving and flags the ones stuck too long.
- **Permit authority tracking with contact details** — Puts the right permit-office information at the team's fingertips.
- **Permit fees that auto-post to job costs** — Captures permitting cost against the job automatically.
- **Engineering sub-workflow (firm, type, status, cost, documents)** — Tracks structural/electrical/mechanical engineering on jobs that need it.
- **Module on/off toggle** — Companies that don't pull permits can hide the whole area.

## 13. Sales Motivation (Campaigns & Leaderboard)

- **Sales campaigns / competitions (KPI, period, ranked reward tiers)** — Drives sales performance with structured, measurable contests.
- **Flexible rewards ($ bonus, % of GP or sales, or non-cash award)** — Lets owners incentivize exactly the behavior they want.
- **Live standings with auto-refresh** — Keeps competition visible and motivating in real time.
- **Campaign payouts that push to payroll** — Turns a won competition into a paid bonus with no extra admin.
- **Leaderboard rankings (value, deals, profit, margin) by period** — Creates healthy competition and recognizes top performers.
- **Achievement badges and rank-movement indicators** — Gamifies performance and rewards improvement, not just absolute numbers.
- **Per-member stat sheets (PM sales sheets, technician labor sheets)** — Gives each person a clear view of their own contribution.

## 14. Analytics & Reporting

- **Multi-tab analytics workspace (sales, profitability, production, budget, labor, commissions, sources, and more)** — Answers virtually any business question without exporting to a spreadsheet.
- **Gross profit and margin analysis by PM, job type, and lead type** — Reveals what work and which people are actually profitable.
- **Sold-to-installed follow-through and cancellation reasons** — Shows how much sold work is being lost and why.
- **Pipeline funnel and revenue forecast (pipeline value × win rate)** — Predicts future revenue from the current pipeline.
- **Production analytics (job health, delays, QC pass rate, schedule accuracy, crew utilization, backlog)** — Gives operations managers a full picture of delivery performance.
- **Lead-source and conversion-funnel analytics (joins CRM appointments with jobs)** — Proves which marketing sources produce real, closed revenue.
- **Printable PDF report and multi-sheet Excel export** — Produces board-ready and accountant-ready reports in one click.
- **Per-project Excel workbook (summary + one tab per job)** — Delivers a complete, itemized record for any timeframe.

## 15. Configuration & Templates

- **Company branding (name and logo)** — Makes the platform feel like the company's own tool.
- **Configurable financial rates (blended labor rate, standard margin, commission tiers, bonus rules)** — Tailors every calculation to the company's actual economics.
- **Workflow templates by job type (auto-generate tasks, required documents, QC checkpoints)** — Standardizes how every job type is run, so quality doesn't depend on memory.
- **Production-readiness, phase, and payment-draw templates** — Applies the company's best-practice playbook to every new job automatically.
- **Custom categories (job types, labor categories, spending categories, contact sources, appointment types)** — Fits the software to the business's own language and process.
- **Custom fields (11 types, placement, visibility, required, job-type scoping)** — Tracks anything unique to the business on the job card.
- **CRM custom-field import with two-way sync** — Avoids re-entering data that already lives in the CRM.
- **Configurable notifications (team, customer, on-my-way distance, aging alerts, end-of-day report)** — Keeps the right people informed automatically, on the company's terms.
- **AI-insight end-of-day analytics email** — Delivers a smart daily readout to managers without anyone building it.

## 16. Roles, Access & Multi-Company

- **Role-based access (Owner, Admin, PM, Technician, Payroll, plus agency admins)** — Ensures each person sees exactly what they should — no more, no less.
- **Per-user permission overrides across 19 menus** — Fine-tunes access for unusual roles without workarounds.
- **Row-level data scoping (PMs see their jobs; techs see their own time/spending)** — Protects sensitive data and keeps each person focused.
- **"View as" impersonation for agency admins** — Lets support and owners see precisely what a user sees, making troubleshooting and training easy.
- **Administrator-managed logins with password reset** — Keeps account control in the company's hands, with no open self-signup.
- **Multi-tenant agency console (create, open, rename, suspend, delete companies)** — Lets an agency run many contractor businesses from one login, fully walled off.
- **Team management (add members, assign roles, edit profiles, adjusted labor rates, recurring draws)** — Onboards and manages staff in minutes.

## 17. Support & Knowledge Base

- **In-app support tickets (bug/support) with two-way chat** — Gives users a direct help channel without leaving the app.
- **Agency-side ticket triage queue** — Lets the provider support all client companies from one place.
- **Searchable knowledge base with a Markdown editor (global or company-scoped)** — Puts SOPs and help articles where the team works.
- **"Ask AI" assistant grounded in the knowledge base** — Answers how-to questions instantly, deflecting support load.

## 18. Integrations

- **GoHighLevel (GHL) CRM sync** — Pulls deals and appointments in automatically, so sales activity flows into operations with no double entry.
- **QuickBooks Online sync (costs, optional revenue, scheduled auto-sync)** — Keeps the books current without manual expense entry.
- **Gusto payroll integration** — Turns approved commissions into staged payroll lines, ready for the company to run.
- **Wave accounting integration** — Posts costs and revenue as clean double-entry bookkeeping.
- **Google Drive integration (auto per-job folders, two-way sync)** — Keeps job photos and documents organized in Drive automatically.
- **CompanyCam integration (auto photo import)** — Brings field photos in without anyone uploading them manually.
- **CSV import across records (leads, vendors, jobs)** — Makes migrating existing data fast and painless.

## 19. Platform & Security

- **Installable web app (PWA) for phone, tablet, and desktop** — One tool that works everywhere, on any device, with an app-like experience.
- **Real server-enforced cloud login** — Genuine security, not a client-side gate, so business data is actually protected.
- **Row Level Security on all data** — Each company's data is isolated at the database level and never left exposed.
- **Private receipt storage via short-lived signed links** — Keeps financial documents secure while remaining easy to access.
- **Transactional email (reminders, sign-in links) from a verified domain** — Reliable, professional communication out of the box.
- **Public technician profile pages ("who's coming")** — Builds customer trust before the tech arrives.
- **JSON backup/restore and data export** — Gives the company full ownership and portability of its data.

## Top-line value themes (for headlines and positioning)

- **One system, whole lifecycle** — Replaces the patchwork of spreadsheets, calendars, and disconnected apps with a single platform from lead to paid commission.
- **Real-time profit visibility** — Know the true margin on every job as it happens, not after it's too late to fix.
- **Built for the field** — Everything a crew needs works from a phone: clock in, log the day, capture costs, snap photos, collect the draw.
- **Automated, accurate pay** — Commissions, splits, bonuses, draws, and clawbacks calculated automatically and staged into payroll.
- **Connected to your stack** — Works with GoHighLevel, QuickBooks, Gusto, Wave, Google Drive, and CompanyCam.
- **Multi-company ready** — Agencies and multi-brand operators run every company from one login, fully isolated.
- **Secure and owned** — Real authentication, database-level isolation, and full data export.
